Future Work Rules

Operational guardrails for every future Guild Hall, roster, or patch update.

Do not redraw background

Use the approved Guild Hall background asset only. Keep the background untouched in all future patches.

Use approved transparent cutouts from cropped_sprites/front_cutouts/. No new character redraws.

Start Guild Hall edits from v11

Use poster/01_guild_hall_composite_v11.png and poster/01_guild_hall_composite_v11_labeled.png as the baseline.

Keep role mapping from the bible

Role assignments must stay aligned to composition_bible.html and the approved Guild Bible canon.
